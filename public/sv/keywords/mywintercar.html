MyWinterCar - Complete Game Guide

MyWinterCar (often abbreviated as MWC) is the popular shorthand name for My Winter Car, the Finnish winter survival and car building simulation game by Amistech Games.

Om MyWinterCar

MyWinterCar is set in rural Finland during winter 1992. Players take on the role of a young person who must rebuild the Corris Rivett car while surviving the harsh Finnish winter. The game is the spiritual successor to My Summer Car.

  • Use the sauna regularly to warm up
  • Stock up on food before long trips
  • Scrape your car windows before driving
  • Save your game progress frequently
  • Check the phone for job opportunities
