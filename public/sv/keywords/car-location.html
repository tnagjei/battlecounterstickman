The Corris Rivett car body is located in the garage area near your house. Here's exactly where to find it.

Main Car Body Location

The car body is in the garage attached to or near your house. Start the game and explore the property to find the garage.

Finding Parts

  • Garage: Main body and many large parts
  • Shelves: Smaller components
  • Boxes: Various items
  • Property: Some parts scattered around
