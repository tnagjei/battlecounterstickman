Om Corris Rivett

The Corris Rivett is a fictional Finnish car that serves as your main project in the game. It needs to be assembled from scratch using parts found around the game world.

Bildelar

  • Engine: The heart of the car
  • Body: External panels and frame
  • Interior: Seats, dashboard, controls
  • Wheels: Tires and suspension
  • Electrical: Lights, battery, wiring

Byggtips

Start with the engine and work outward. Keep your workspace warm and don't forget to maintain your survival needs while building.
