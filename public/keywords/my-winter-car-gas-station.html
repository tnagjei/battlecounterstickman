Complete Gas Station Guide

The gas station is an essential location in My Winter Car. It provides fuel for your car and other vehicles, along with various supplies and services. Knowing how to use the gas station efficiently helps you stay mobile and complete your tasks.

How to Find the Gas Station

The gas station is located on the main road that runs through the game world. From your house, follow the main road toward town. The station is visible from the road with its distinctive pumps and signage. You can also use map mods to pinpoint the exact location.

Refueling Your Car

To refuel your Corris Rivett, park near the pumps. Get out and interact with the fuel pump. Insert the nozzle into your car's fuel tank and pump the fuel. Watch the meter to control how much you purchase. Payment is handled at the station counter. Keep spare cash for fuel expenses.

Available Services

  • Gasoline: Primary fuel for your Corris Rivett
  • Motor Oil: For engine maintenance
  • Brake Fluid: Essential for brake system
  • Coolant: Keep your engine from overheating
  • Snacks: Quick food for survival needs

Fuel Management Tips

  • Fill up before long trips to avoid running out
  • Carry a spare jerry can for emergencies
  • Monitor your fuel gauge regularly
  • Plan routes to include fuel stops
  • Budget for fuel costs when calculating job profits

Emergency Fuel Situations

If you run out of fuel away from the station, you'll need to walk back or use another vehicle. Keep a jerry can at home filled with gasoline as a backup. Running out of fuel in remote areas during winter can be dangerous due to cold exposure.

FAQ

Q: Where exactly is the gas station?

A: On the main road between your house and town - look for the fuel pumps.

Q: How much does fuel cost?

A: Fuel prices are per liter - watch the pump meter for cost as you fill.

Q: What if I run out of gas?

A: You'll need to walk to get a jerry can or find another vehicle.
