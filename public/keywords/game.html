My Winter Car Game - Complete Overview & Features Guide

My Winter Car is a survival and car building simulation game developed by Amistech Games. Set in rural Finland during winter 1992, players must rebuild the Corris Rivett car while surviving harsh winter conditions.

Key Features

  • Car Building: Assemble over 200 parts to build your vehicle
  • Survival: Manage hunger, fatigue, and body temperature
  • Open World: Explore the Finnish countryside
  • Jobs: Earn money through various employment options
  • Realistic Mechanics: Authentic car building experience

Gameplay Elements

The game combines mechanical simulation with survival gameplay. Players must balance their time between working on the car, earning money, and staying alive in the freezing Finnish winter. The day-night cycle and weather systems add additional challenges, requiring careful planning for outdoor activities.

Survival Mechanics

Survival in My Winter Car requires managing several key stats:

  • Body Temperature: Stay warm using the sauna, heaters, and proper clothing
  • Hunger: Eat regularly from your food supplies or the local store
  • Fatigue: Sleep in your bed to restore energy
  • Stress: Manage stress levels through various activities

Economy and Jobs

Money is essential for buying car parts and supplies. Work various jobs including:

  • Taxi driver - Transport passengers around the map
  • Firewood delivery - Chop and deliver wood to customers
  • Mail delivery - Distribute mail throughout the region
  • Garbage collection - Help keep the town clean

Early Access Status

My Winter Car is currently in Early Access on Steam, meaning the game is still in active development with regular updates and new features being added. The developers actively engage with the community for feedback and suggestions.

System Requirements

To run My Winter Car smoothly, you'll need a modern PC with at least 8GB RAM, a dedicated graphics card, and Windows 10 or later. Check the Steam store page for detailed minimum and recommended specifications.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is My Winter Car free to play?

A: No, My Winter Car is a paid game available on Steam. However, there are occasionally sales and discounts.

Q: Is this game related to My Summer Car?

A: Yes, My Winter Car is the spiritual successor to My Summer Car, made by the same development team Amistech Games.

Q: When will the full version be released?

A: The game is currently in Early Access with no confirmed full release date. Development continues with regular updates.
