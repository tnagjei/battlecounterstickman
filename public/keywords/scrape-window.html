My Winter Car Scrape Window - Ice Removal Guide

Learn how to scrape ice and snow from your car windows in My Winter Car for safe driving.

How to Scrape Windows

  1. Find an ice scraper tool
  2. Approach the icy window
  3. Use the scraper to remove ice
  4. Repeat for all windows

Why It's Important

Icy windows block your view while driving, making it dangerous. Always clear your windows before driving.

Complete Window Scraping Guide

Scraping windows is a critical daily task in My Winter Car during the Finnish winter. Ice and frost accumulate on your car windows overnight and even during short parking periods. This comprehensive guide teaches you everything about maintaining clear windows for safe driving.

When to Scrape Windows

You should scrape your windows before every drive during winter. Ice forms quickly in sub-zero temperatures. Even if you only parked for a short time, check all windows. Morning drives typically require the most scraping as ice builds up overnight.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Locate your ice scraper tool
  2. Approach the frozen window you want to clear
  3. Equip the scraper and interact with the window
  4. Hold the interaction button while scraping
  5. Watch the ice disappear from the glass
  6. Move to the next window and repeat

Which Windows to Clear

  • Windshield: Most important - your primary view
  • Rear Window: Essential for reversing and checking behind
  • Side Windows: Important for checking blind spots
  • Mirrors: Often ice over and need clearing

Window Maintenance Tips

  • Use the car heater to help defrost from inside
  • Scrape thoroughly - partial visibility is still dangerous
  • Store the scraper in your car for easy access
  • Check windows even after short stops
  • Consider parking in covered areas when possible

Dangers of Not Scraping

Driving with icy windows significantly reduces your ability to see the road, other vehicles, and obstacles. In the dark Finnish winter, this becomes even more dangerous. Never drive until all windows are properly cleared.

FAQ

Q: How do I scrape windows?

A: Equip the ice scraper and interact with frozen windows to clear them.

Q: Do I need to scrape every time?

A: Yes, ice forms quickly in Finnish winter - always check before driving.

Q: Can the heater defrost windows?

A: The heater helps but scraping is faster and more effective for thick ice.
