My Winter Car Parts Guide

The Corris Rivett requires over 200 parts to complete. This guide covers where to find them and how to order new components.

Parts Categories

  • Engine: Block, head, pistons, valves, timing components
  • Transmission: Gearbox, clutch, driveshaft
  • Suspension: Struts, springs, control arms
  • Brakes: Rotors, calipers, pads, lines
  • Electrical: Battery, alternator, wiring, lights
  • Body: Panels, doors, windows, trim

Where to Get Parts

  • Parts Catalog: Order new parts via phone
  • Junkyard: Find used parts at lower prices
  • Barn: Some parts already with the car
  • Around Map: Scattered components to find

Ordering Tips

Check the parts catalog in your mailbox. Call the number to place orders. Parts arrive in 1-3 game days depending on size and availability.

Part Quality Levels

Parts come in different quality levels that affect performance and price:

  • New Parts: Best quality, highest price from catalog
  • Used Parts: Cheaper from junkyard but may have wear
  • Free Parts: Found around the map, variable condition

Organizing Your Parts

Keep your workshop organized by grouping parts together. Create separate areas for engine components, body panels, and electrical parts. This makes assembly faster and prevents losing small items.

Parts by System Category

Understanding parts by their system helps with organized building. Each system must be complete before the car functions properly. Missing even one small part can prevent entire systems from working.

Engine System Parts

The engine requires the block, pistons, crankshaft, camshaft, valves, and timing components. External parts include the carburetor, intake manifold, exhaust manifold, water pump, alternator, starter motor, and distributor. Total engine parts exceed 40 individual components.

Suspension and Brake Parts

Front suspension includes struts, control arms, and steering components. Rear suspension uses springs and shock absorbers. The brake system requires rotors, calipers, pads, lines, and the master cylinder. Wheels and tires complete the rolling chassis.

Electrical System Parts

The electrical system includes the battery, alternator, wiring harness, fuse box, ignition switch, starter relay, headlights, taillights, turn signals, instrument cluster, and various switches. Proper wiring is essential for the car to operate.

Where to Find Free Parts

  • The Barn: Many parts are already with the car
  • Around the Property: Check garage and outbuildings
  • Map Exploration: Some parts scattered in the game world
  • Junkyard: Cheap used parts available

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How many total parts does the car need?

A: The complete Corris Rivett requires over 200 individual parts across all systems.

Q: Where can I find the parts catalog?

A: The parts catalog arrives in your mailbox at the start of the game.

Q: Are used parts safe to use?

A: Yes, but they may have reduced durability. Use new parts for critical components like brakes.
