Corris Rivett My Winter Car - Complete Car Build Guide

The Corris Rivett is your project car in My Winter Car. Build it from 200+ parts to survive the Finnish winter.

About the Corris Rivett

The Corris Rivett is a fictional Finnish car that serves as your main project in the game. It needs to be assembled from scratch using parts found around the game world.

Car Components

  • Engine: The heart of the car
  • Body: External panels and frame
  • Interior: Seats, dashboard, controls
  • Wheels: Tires and suspension
  • Electrical: Lights, battery, wiring

Building Tips

Start with the engine and work outward. Keep your workspace warm and don't forget to maintain your survival needs while building.

Getting Started with the Corris Rivett

When you first start the game, the Corris Rivett chassis is located in the barn near your house. The car is completely disassembled and requires you to find and install over 200 individual parts. Many parts can be found scattered around the game world, while others must be ordered from the parts catalog using the phone.

Recommended Assembly Order

  • Step 1: Start with the chassis and suspension components
  • Step 2: Install the engine block and internal components
  • Step 3: Connect the transmission and drivetrain
  • Step 4: Add the fuel system and electrical wiring
  • Step 5: Install body panels, seats, and interior
  • Step 6: Mount wheels and perform final checks

Common Issues and Solutions

Many new players struggle with the car not starting after assembly. Check these common issues:

  • Ensure the battery is charged and properly connected
  • Verify all fuel lines are connected
  • Check that the spark plugs are installed
  • Make sure the ignition key is in the on position

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Where is the Corris Rivett located?

A: The Corris Rivett is in the barn next to your house at the start of the game. Look for the large wooden structure with the car chassis inside.

Q: How many parts does the Corris Rivett need?

A: The complete car requires over 200 individual parts including engine components, body panels, electrical systems, and interior elements.

Q: Why won't my Corris Rivett start?

A: Common causes include dead battery, missing fuel, disconnected spark plugs, or incomplete wiring. Check each system systematically.
