Complete Ice Scraper Guide

The ice scraper is an essential winter tool in My Winter Car. Finnish winters leave your car windows covered in ice and frost, blocking visibility and making driving dangerous. This guide explains where to find the scraper and how to use it effectively.

Where to Find the Ice Scraper

The ice scraper can be found in several locations around your property. Check inside the garage near your other tools. Look on shelves, workbenches, and in storage areas. It may also be inside your house or near the car itself. The scraper is a small handheld tool.

Possible Scraper Locations

  • Garage: On workbenches or shelves
  • Barn: Near other tools
  • House: Inside storage areas
  • Near Car: On the ground beside the Corris Rivett

Using the Ice Scraper

  1. Pick up the ice scraper with the interact button
  2. Approach a window covered in ice or frost
  3. Use the scraper on the window to remove ice
  4. Continue scraping until the window is clear
  5. Repeat for all windows - windshield, rear, and side windows

Window Scraping Tips

  • Scrape all windows before driving for full visibility
  • Start with the windshield as it's most important
  • Don't forget side mirrors if they're icy
  • Keep the scraper in your car for emergency situations
  • Running the car heater helps defrost from inside

Why Ice Removal Matters

Driving with icy windows is extremely dangerous. You won't be able to see obstacles, other vehicles, or the road clearly. In the harsh Finnish winter, ice accumulates quickly even after parking briefly. Always ensure clear visibility before driving.

FAQ

Q: Where is the ice scraper?

A: Check your garage, barn, or inside the house near storage areas.

Q: Can I buy an ice scraper?

A: The scraper is already on your property - search storage areas carefully.

Q: How long does scraping take?

A: A few seconds per window, but it varies with ice thickness.
