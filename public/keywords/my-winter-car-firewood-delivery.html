My Winter Car Firewood Delivery

Learn how to earn money through firewood delivery in My Winter Car.

How It Works

  • Chop wood using axe
  • Load wood onto vehicle or sled
  • Deliver to customers
  • Collect payment

Tips

Firewood delivery is great for beginners as it doesn't require a working car. Bundle multiple deliveries for efficiency.

Complete Firewood Guide

Firewood delivery is the ideal starting job in My Winter Car. You can begin earning money immediately without needing a working vehicle. This guide covers everything you need to know about becoming a successful firewood delivery person.

Finding and Chopping Wood

The main wood supply is located at the log pile near your house. Take the axe from your workshop and head to the chopping block. Position a log on the block and swing the axe to split it into firewood pieces. Each log produces multiple pieces of usable firewood.

Delivery Methods

Without a car, you can use a sled or wheelbarrow to transport firewood. Load the wood onto your transport method and haul it to the customer's location. A sled works well in snow, while a wheelbarrow is better for cleared paths. Once you have a working car, you can load wood into the trunk for larger deliveries.

Getting Customers

Customers call your phone to request firewood deliveries. Answer the call, note the address and amount needed, then prepare and deliver their order. Payment is made upon successful delivery. Repeat customers may order regularly, providing steady income.

Earnings Breakdown

  • Small load: 20-30 marks (quick delivery)
  • Medium load: 35-50 marks (standard order)
  • Large load: 60-80 marks (requires vehicle)

Pro Tips for Efficiency

  • Chop wood in advance and stockpile it
  • Group nearby deliveries together
  • Dress warmly while chopping outdoors
  • Work during daylight for safety
  • Use a car for large orders once available

FAQ

Q: Where do I find the axe?

A: The axe is located in your workshop near the house. Look around the tool area.

Q: How much can I earn daily?

A: Expect 100-200 marks per day with consistent work chopping and delivering.

Q: Is this job available all year?

A: Yes, firewood delivery is available year-round, though demand increases in winter.
