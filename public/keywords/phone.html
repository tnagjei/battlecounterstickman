My Winter Car Phone - How to Use the Phone Guide

The phone is an essential tool in My Winter Car. Use it to order parts, call for services, and interact with NPCs.

Phone Location

The phone is located in your house. Look for it on the wall or table. Interact with it to access the dialing interface.

How to Use

  1. Approach the phone and interact
  2. Check the phone book for numbers
  3. Dial the number you need
  4. Wait for the call to connect

What You Can Do

  • Order Parts: Call shops to order car parts
  • Call Taxi: Request a ride
  • Jobs: Contact employers for work

Complete Phone Guide

The phone is one of the most important tools in My Winter Car. Located in your house, it connects you to shops, services, and job opportunities. Mastering the phone system is essential for progressing in the game.

Finding the Phone

The phone is located inside your house, typically mounted on the wall or placed on a table. When you first start the game, take time to locate it as you'll need it frequently. Look for the classic rotary-style phone design.

Important Phone Numbers

  • Parts Shop: Order car parts from the catalog
  • Taxi Service: Call for a ride when your car isn't running
  • Employment Office: Find and accept job opportunities
  • Store: Check availability and prices
  • Gas Station: Inquire about services

Ordering Parts by Phone

  1. Find the item you need in the parts catalog book
  2. Note the part number and price
  3. Call the parts shop using the phone
  4. Provide the part number when prompted
  5. Confirm the order and payment method
  6. Wait for delivery to your mailbox

Phone Tips

  • Keep a written list of frequently used numbers
  • Check the phone book for all available contacts
  • Call during business hours for most services
  • Some calls require payment - ensure you have funds

FAQ

Q: Where is the phone?

A: In your house - check the walls and tables near the main living area.

Q: How do I find phone numbers?

A: Check the phone book near the phone or in-game menus for contacts.

Q: When do parts arrive?

A: Parts ordered by phone are delivered to your mailbox within 1-2 game days.
