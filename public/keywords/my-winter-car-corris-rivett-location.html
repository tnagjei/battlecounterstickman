Corris Rivett Location in My Winter Car

Looking for the Corris Rivett? This guide shows you exactly where to find your project car and its parts in My Winter Car.

Finding the Corris Rivett

The Corris Rivett is located in the barn near your house. When you start the game, exit your house and walk toward the large red barn building. Inside you'll find the disassembled Rivett waiting for restoration.

Parts Locations

  • Barn: Main car body, chassis, and many pre-installed components
  • Garage shelves: Tools and some small parts
  • Around the house: Scattered components like batteries
  • Junkyard: Used parts at discount prices
  • Parts catalog: Order new parts by phone

Important Locations

  • Mailbox: Receive parts catalog and ordered components
  • Phone: Call to order parts from Rivett dealer
  • Gas station: Buy fuel, oil, and basic supplies
