My Winter Car Rivett Number

Looking for the phone number to order Rivett parts? This guide covers all important phone numbers in My Winter Car.

Ordering Parts by Phone

Use the phone in your house to call the parts dealer. The number is listed in your parts catalog magazine. Follow the prompts to order the components you need.

How to Use the Phone

  • Pick up the phone handset
  • Dial the number from your catalog
  • Wait for connection
  • Place your order verbally

Important Numbers

Keep a list of important numbers near your phone: parts dealer, taxi dispatch, and emergency services. The parts catalog contains the main ordering number.
