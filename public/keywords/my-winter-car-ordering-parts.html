My Winter Car Ordering Parts

Learn how to order parts for your Corris Rivett in My Winter Car.

How to Order

  • Find parts catalog in mailbox
  • Note part numbers and prices
  • Use house phone to call dealer
  • Place order with part numbers
  • Wait for delivery at mailbox

Delivery Time

Parts typically arrive in 1-3 game days depending on size.
