My Winter Car Taxi Job

Guide to becoming a taxi driver in My Winter Car.

Requirements

  • Working car (Corris Rivett)
  • Fuel in tank
  • Valid driver (you)

How to Start

Call taxi dispatch via phone, pick up passengers, drive them to destinations, collect payment.

Complete Taxi Guide

The taxi job is the highest-paying work in My Winter Car, but it requires a fully functional car. Once you have completed your Corris Rivett build and have it running reliably, taxi driving becomes your best income source.

Getting Started as a Taxi Driver

To begin taxi work, call the taxi dispatch number found in your phone book. They will give you information about available fares in the area. Drive to the pickup location, wait for the passenger to enter your vehicle, and drive them to their requested destination. Payment is collected upon arrival.

Fare Pricing System

Taxi fares are calculated based on distance traveled. Short trips within town typically pay 30-50 marks, while longer trips to distant locations can pay 100-200 marks or more. The key to maximizing income is completing as many fares as possible while minimizing fuel consumption and travel time between pickups.

Vehicle Requirements

  • Fully assembled and running Corris Rivett
  • Sufficient fuel in the tank
  • Working headlights for night driving
  • Reliable brakes for safe stops
  • Heater for passenger comfort in winter

Maximizing Earnings

  • Learn the map to find fastest routes
  • Keep your fuel tank full to avoid running out mid-fare
  • Drive carefully to avoid crash damage and repair costs
  • Accept fares that are close to your current location
  • Work during busy hours for more available passengers

Common Problems

Taxi drivers often face challenges like running out of fuel mid-trip, vehicle breakdowns, and getting lost on unfamiliar routes. Keep a jerry can of extra fuel in your trunk and learn the main roads before taking distant fares.

FAQ

Q: How much can I earn per day?

A: With consistent work, expect 300-600 marks daily from taxi driving.

Q: Do I need a special license?

A: No license required - just a working car and the taxi dispatch phone number.

Q: What if my car breaks down during a fare?

A: The passenger will leave and you'll lose the fare. Maintain your car regularly to prevent this.
