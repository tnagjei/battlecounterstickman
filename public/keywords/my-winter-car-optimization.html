My Winter Car Optimization Guide

Running into performance issues? This guide helps you optimize My Winter Car for better FPS and smoother gameplay.

In-Game Settings

  • Resolution: Lower resolution for significant FPS boost
  • Shadow Quality: Reduce from High to Medium or Low
  • Draw Distance: Decrease for better performance
  • Anti-Aliasing: Disable or use FXAA instead of MSAA

System Optimization

  • Update graphics drivers to latest version
  • Close background applications
  • Disable Steam overlay if experiencing stutters
  • Run game in windowed borderless mode

Minimum vs Recommended

MWC requires decent hardware due to the open world nature. For smooth 60fps, aim for specs above the minimum requirements, especially RAM and GPU.
