My Winter Car Job Guide

Need money for car parts? This guide covers all available jobs in My Winter Car and how to maximize your earnings.

Available Jobs

  • Firewood Delivery: Chop wood and deliver to customers. Good steady income.
  • Taxi Driver: Pick up passengers and drive them to destinations. Requires working car.
  • Mailer: Deliver mail around the countryside.
  • Log Machine: Operate heavy machinery for forestry work.

Best Jobs for Beginners

Start with firewood delivery as it requires no vehicle. Once you have a working car, taxi jobs offer better pay per time invested.

Tips for Earning More

  • Complete jobs efficiently to maximize hourly earnings
  • Combine multiple deliveries when possible
  • Keep your vehicle maintained to avoid breakdowns
  • Learn the map to find faster routes
