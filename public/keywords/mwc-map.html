MWC Map - My Winter Car Navigation Guide

Navigate the Finnish countryside with a map. Learn about map mods and key locations in My Winter Car.

Map Overview

The game world includes your house, shops, job locations, and other points of interest. Explore to learn the layout.

Key Locations

  • Your house and garage
  • Shops and stores
  • Job locations
  • NPC homes

Complete Map Guide

The My Winter Car game world is set in rural Finland during winter. The map includes various locations essential for survival, work, and car building. Learning the layout helps you navigate efficiently and complete tasks faster.

Your Home Base

Your home is located in a rural area and serves as your main base of operations. The house contains your bed, kitchen, and phone. The barn nearby holds the Corris Rivett chassis. The sauna building is essential for warming up. Get familiar with this area first.

Town and Shops

The nearby town contains essential services. The grocery store sells food, drinks, and basic supplies. The parts shop is where you order catalog items. The gas station provides fuel for your car and other vehicles. Learn the route to town early.

Job Locations

  • Taxi Pickup: Various locations around the map
  • Firewood Customers: Homes throughout the area
  • Mail Route: Mailboxes along roads
  • Employment Office: In town for main jobs

Navigation Tips

  • Follow main roads to avoid getting lost
  • Use landmarks like buildings and signs
  • Note fuel stations for your car
  • Learn shortcuts between common destinations
  • Consider map mods for visual reference

Map Mods

Several community mods add in-game maps or GPS systems. Check NexusMods for available options. These mods can display your location and destinations, making navigation much easier especially when starting out.

FAQ

Q: Is there an in-game map?

A: Not by default, but map mods are available on NexusMods.

Q: How do I find the store?

A: Follow the main road from your house - the store is in the nearby town.

Q: Where is the gas station?

A: The gas station is located on the main road near town.
