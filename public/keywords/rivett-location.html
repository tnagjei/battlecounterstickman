My Winter Car Rivett Location - Find Your Car

The Corris Rivett is located in the garage. Here's exactly where to find it.

Exact Location

The car body is in your garage, near your house. When you start the game, explore the property to find the garage building.

Finding Parts

  • Check shelves and boxes in the garage
  • Look around the property
  • Order missing parts by phone

Complete Location Guide

Finding the Corris Rivett is your first task in My Winter Car. The car is disassembled and waiting in the barn near your house. This guide explains exactly where to find the chassis and how to locate all the scattered parts.

Finding the Car Chassis

When you start the game, walk outside your house and look for the large barn structure. The Corris Rivett chassis is inside this barn, sitting on the floor. The car is completely stripped down - just the bare frame waiting for you to rebuild it piece by piece.

Parts Around Your Property

Many parts are scattered around your property and inside the barn. Check all shelves, workbenches, and storage areas. Some parts are on the ground or in boxes. Interior components are often in the house. Engine parts may be on separate workbenches in the barn.

Where to Find Specific Parts

  • Engine Block: Usually on a workbench in the barn
  • Body Panels: Leaning against walls or on shelves
  • Wheels: Often stored in corners of the barn
  • Interior Parts: Check inside your house
  • Small Parts: In boxes and on workbenches

Missing Parts

Not all parts can be found on your property. Some must be ordered from the parts catalog using your phone. Check the catalog carefully and note what you need to order. Parts arrive by mail after ordering.

Parts Search Tips

  • Explore every corner of the barn thoroughly
  • Check under tables and behind objects
  • Look in all boxes and storage containers
  • Make a list of what you find vs what you need
  • Order missing parts early to avoid delays

FAQ

Q: Where is the car exactly?

A: In the barn near your house - walk outside and look for the large building.

Q: Are all parts on the property?

A: No, some parts must be ordered from the catalog via phone.

Q: Where is the engine?

A: Usually on a workbench in the barn - look for a separate area with engine parts.
