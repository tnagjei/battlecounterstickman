My Winter Car Parts Magazine - Complete Ordering Guide

The parts magazine is your primary source for ordering new components for the Corris Rivett in My Winter Car. This guide explains how to use the catalog effectively.

Finding the Parts Magazine

The parts catalog arrives in your mailbox at the start of the game. Check your mailbox regularly as new editions may arrive with updated parts and prices. The magazine contains all available new parts for your Rivett build.

How to Order Parts

Ordering from the magazine involves several steps:

  • Open the catalog: Find it in your inventory or on a table
  • Browse categories: Parts are organized by vehicle system
  • Fill out the order form: Write down part numbers and quantities
  • Pay by phone: Call the listed number to place your order
  • Wait for delivery: Parts arrive by mail or delivery truck

Parts Categories Available

The magazine includes these main categories:

  • Engine Components: Pistons, gaskets, timing belts, filters
  • Suspension Parts: Struts, springs, bushings, bearings
  • Brake System: Pads, rotors, calipers, brake lines
  • Electrical: Alternator, starter, wiring harnesses, lights
  • Body Panels: Fenders, doors, hood, trunk lid
  • Interior: Seats, dashboard parts, steering wheel

Delivery Times

Delivery times vary based on several factors:

  • Small parts: 1-2 in-game days
  • Large components: 2-3 in-game days
  • Special order items: Up to 5 in-game days
  • Weather conditions may affect delivery

Ordering Tips

Maximize efficiency with these magazine ordering tips:

  • Plan ahead - order parts before you need them
  • Combine orders to save on delivery fees
  • Keep the catalog somewhere accessible
  • Compare prices with the junkyard for used alternatives
