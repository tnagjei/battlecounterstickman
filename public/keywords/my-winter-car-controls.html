My Winter Car Controls

Complete guide to keyboard and controller controls in My Winter Car.

Keyboard Controls

  • WASD: Movement
  • E: Interact
  • Mouse: Look around
  • Scroll: Tool selection
  • Tab/I: Inventory
  • Esc: Menu

Driving Controls

WASD for steering and acceleration. Use mouse to look around while driving.
