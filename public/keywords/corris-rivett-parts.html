Corris Rivett Parts

Complete parts list for building the Corris Rivett in My Winter Car.

Main Categories

  • Engine: Block, head, pistons, valves
  • Drivetrain: Transmission, clutch, axles
  • Suspension: Struts, springs, bushings
  • Brakes: Rotors, calipers, pads
  • Body: Panels, doors, trim

Complete Parts List

The Corris Rivett requires over 200 individual parts to be fully assembled. These range from small bolts to major components like the engine block and body panels. Some parts are found in the game world while others must be ordered.

Parts by Category

  • Engine: ~40 parts including block, head, and internals
  • Transmission: ~15 parts for gearbox and clutch
  • Suspension: ~25 parts for wheels and steering
  • Body: ~30 parts for panels and trim
  • Electrical: ~20 parts for lights and wiring
  • Interior: ~15 parts for seats and dashboard

Finding Parts

Check the barn, garage, and around the property for initial parts. Use the parts catalog to order what you can't find. The junkyard offers cheaper used parts for non-critical components.

Detailed Parts Inventory

The Corris Rivett is a complete car project requiring extensive parts collection. Understanding the full parts list helps you plan your build and budget your money effectively. Some parts are critical while others are optional upgrades.

Engine Components (40+ parts)

The engine system includes the block, cylinder head, pistons, connecting rods, crankshaft, camshaft, valves, valve springs, pushrods, rocker arms, timing chain, oil pump, water pump, thermostat, carburetor, intake manifold, exhaust manifold, alternator, starter motor, and distributor.

Transmission and Drivetrain (15+ parts)

This system includes the gearbox, clutch disc, pressure plate, flywheel, throwout bearing, driveshaft, differential, axle shafts, CV joints, shift linkage, and gear lever. The transmission connects the engine power to the wheels efficiently.

Suspension and Steering (25+ parts)

Components include front suspension struts, rear leaf springs, shock absorbers, control arms, ball joints, tie rod ends, steering rack, steering column, wheel bearings, brake rotors, brake calipers, brake pads, master cylinder, brake lines, and wheels with tires.

Body and Exterior (30+ parts)

The body consists of doors, hood, trunk lid, front fenders, rear fenders, bumpers, windshield, side windows, rear window, side mirrors, headlights, taillights, turn signals, door handles, hinges, weatherstripping, and various trim pieces.

Interior and Electrical (35+ parts)

Interior parts include front seats, rear seat, dashboard assembly, steering wheel, instrument cluster, switches, wiring harness, fuse box, battery, headlight relay, horn, radio, heater core, blower motor, and various buttons and control knobs.

Parts Acquisition Strategy

  • Free Parts: Search the barn and surrounding property first
  • Junkyard: Best value for used non-critical parts
  • Catalog: New parts for critical components like brakes
  • Priority: Order engine and suspension parts first

FAQ

Q: How many parts total?

A: The complete Corris Rivett requires over 200 individual parts.

Q: Where do I find rare parts?

A: Order from the catalog via phone or check the junkyard for used alternatives.
