My Winter Car Wiki - Community Resource & Game Information

This wiki serves as a comprehensive resource for My Winter Car players. Find detailed guides, part locations, survival tips, and community-contributed knowledge.

Wiki Resources

  • Build Guides: Step-by-step car assembly instructions
  • Parts Database: Complete list of all 200+ parts
  • Survival Guide: Tips for staying alive in winter
  • Jobs Guide: Money-making strategies
  • Video Tutorials: Visual guides from the community

Community Contribution

This wiki is community-driven and constantly updated with new information. Join our Discord to contribute tips, report errors, or suggest new content. Every contribution helps the community grow and provides value to new and experienced players alike.

Wiki Categories

Our wiki is organized into several main categories for easy navigation:

  • Car Assembly: Step-by-step instructions for building the Corris Rivett
  • Parts Reference: Complete database of all car parts and their locations
  • Survival Strategies: Tips for managing hunger, temperature, and fatigue
  • Jobs & Economy: Money-making guides and job walkthroughs
  • Locations: Map guides and important locations
  • Mods: Community modifications and installation guides

How to Use This Wiki

New players should start with our Beginner Guide to understand the basic mechanics. Use the navigation menu or search function to find specific topics. Each guide includes step-by-step instructions, screenshots when available, and links to related content.
