My Winter Car Firewood - Heating & Money Guide

Firewood is essential for survival in My Winter Car. Use it to heat your home and sauna, or sell it for money.

Getting Firewood

  • Chop trees with an axe
  • Find logs around the property
  • Deliver firewood for money

Using Firewood

Add firewood to stoves and saunas to generate heat. Keep your home warm to survive the Finnish winter.
