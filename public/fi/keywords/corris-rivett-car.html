Corris Rivett - The My Winter Car Vehicle

The Corris Rivett is the iconic project car that players build in My Winter Car.

A fictional Finnish hatchback from the early 1990s. The car comes in parts that need to be assembled to create a working vehicle capable of surviving harsh winter conditions.

Specifications

  • Type: Compact hatchback
  • Era: Early 1990s
