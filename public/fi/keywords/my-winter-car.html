My Winter Car (MWC) is a survival and car building simulation game developed by Amistech Games. Set in rural Finland during the winter of 1992, players rebuild the iconic Corris Rivett while surviving harsh winter conditions.

What is My Winter Car?

My Winter Car is the spiritual successor to the popular My Summer Car. The game challenges players to restore a classic Finnish car while managing survival mechanics like hunger, fatigue, and body temperature in freezing winter conditions.

Getting Started

New to My Winter Car? Start by familiarizing yourself with the basics:

  • Find the Corris Rivett in the barn
  • Keep warm using the sauna and proper clothing
  • Earn money to buy car parts from the shop
  • Complete the car and explore the countryside

Availability

My Winter Car is available on Steam in Early Access. The game receives regular updates with new features, bug fixes, and content additions.
